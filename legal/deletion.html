Data Deletion Instructions

AI Sales Instagram Integration

Important: Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have backed up any important information before proceeding.

How to delete your data

  1. Send deletion request:
    Email jafer_aliraq@yahoo.com with the subject "Instagram Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include required information:
    • Your Instagram username
    • Your Instagram business account ID (if applicable)
    • Email address associated with the service
    • Confirmation that you want to delete ALL data
  3. Verification process:
    We may ask you to verify ownership of the Instagram account for security purposes
  4. Processing time:
    We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 business days

What data will be deleted

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will permanently delete:

  • All conversation messages and history
  • Your account configuration and settings
  • Analytics data associated with your account
  • Stored business information and preferences
  • AI interaction logs and generated responses

Automatic data deletion

Even without a deletion request, we automatically delete:

  • Conversation data: After 30 days
  • Analytics data: After 90 days
  • Temporary logs: After 7 days

Data that may be retained

In some cases, we may need to retain certain data for legal or security reasons:

  • Financial records (if applicable) - as required by law
  • Security logs - for preventing fraud and abuse
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics - with no personal identifiers

Deletion confirmation

Once your data has been deleted, we will send you a confirmation email. This email will include:

  • Confirmation of deletion completion
  • List of data types that were deleted
  • Date and time of deletion

Questions or issues

If you encounter any issues with the deletion process or have questions:

  • Email: jafer_aliraq@yahoo.com
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Support"

Alternative: Account deactivation

If you want to temporarily stop using the service without deleting your data:

  1. Remove our app from your Instagram business account settings
  2. Contact us to deactivate your account temporarily
  3. Your data will still be automatically deleted according to our retention policies

For more information about our data practices, see our Privacy Policy.
